WHAT IS A PROJECT

Simply put, a project, is exactly what it sounds like. That is, some work we are going to do together. It would probably make sense to have one project per 'room', but its entirely up to you. A project is really a collection of work, schemes, images, tasks, events, chats and anything else that happens during the course of your interior design journey with us.

If you want to talk through a project beforehand, just check out the list of designers online in the list on the left. To start up a chat, just click on a name.

It just gives us a way to keep everything neat and together. You can create as many projects as you want, you only have to submit the ones you want us to help you with. The system is free to use as much as you want, you only pay for our design work!
